the fast art gallery

This is a slightly arbitrary collection of some of my collages.

I entitled this gallery fast art gallery not only because each image was created in less than a couple of hours but also because the word fast means "nearly" or "almost" in German.

Each of the collages is assigned to one of the following categories:

photocollage
A collage I made from photos photographed by myself.
papercollage
A collage I made from images found in advertisings, magazines, brochures, books, etc.
computercollage

Same as above but assembled on the computer.
